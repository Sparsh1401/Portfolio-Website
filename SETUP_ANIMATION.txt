‚öôÔ∏è CUSTOMIZATION:
=================

To adjust animation positions:
1. Open your diagram in any image viewer
2. Note the pixel coordinates of components
3. Edit src/components/AnimatedSystemDiagram.js
4. Update coordinates in:
   - dataFlows array (lines 14-46)
   - serviceNodes array (lines 49-56)

Current coordinates (based on your image):
- Driver API: x=360, y=200
- Kafka: x=680, y=310
- Redis: x=1140, y=190
- Spark: x=970, y=310
